Path Towards increased self-efficacy

Our course is for positive development anchored on creating higher and healthier quality of life and increased self-efficacy. What we do is inspiring people to take actions based on engagements that focus on empowering youths to think more critically, through projects that promote capacity building in individuals.

Different Specialized Portfolios

Skill Aquisition

A.K. Horsfall Foundation has rolled out various technical skill acquisition schemes in areas such as Agriculture, Information Technology (IT), Cinematography, Auto-mechanics, etc. to a good number of beneficiaries.

Craft & Artisans

The task is challenging, but we're determined to touch lives. Our empowerment initiative has also a platform that targets those interested artisans, craft such as Carpentry and Woodwork, Sewing & Tailoring.

Social Development

We place people at the heart of our developmental programs. All efforts we make are meant to benefit people directly, and we work for the well-being of every individuals within and outside our catchment ereas.

Empowering the less-privileged

Intervention that is worth showing.

The A.K. Horsfall Foundation has over the years given scholarships to various indigent undergraduate students and a few secondary school students. This programme is potentially effective but had been limited in scope due to the paucity of funds which has so far come primarily from Chief A.K. Horsfall. Together, we can do more.
