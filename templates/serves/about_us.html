We share the dream of helping people in Niger Delta, Nigeria

This is the thing that inspires us

The A.K Horsfall Foundation carries out its activities in the riverine areas in the Niger Delta region of Nigeria, particularly in Rivers and Bayelsa States .

With its efforts overseen by an independent Board of Trustees, and managed by its president through a leadership team drawn from scholarly and professional disciplines, it is a broad-based charitable organization and trust which has existed for more than a decade.

A.K Horsfall Foundation became truly functional as from 2011 when its chief promoter Chief A.K. Horsfall fully disengaged from the public service into retirement.
